ABOUT US

WHO ARE WE?

We are a unique and specialized hair salon located in the vibrant city of Madison, Wisconsin. Our salon is renowned for its expertise in haircutting, coloring, and styling, and we take pride in providing exceptional services to our valued clients.

In addition to our core services, we also offer professional makeup application and nail care treatments, including both manicures and pedicures. Hair Day has earned a reputation for being at the forefront of hair fashion, particularly for our daring and trendy hair color options and cutting-edge haircut styles.

Our team is dedicated to delivering top-notch services, ensuring that each client leaves our salon feeling confident and satisfied with their new haircut, hairstyle, or nail treatment.

We invite you to schedule an appointment with us to experience our exceptional services firsthand and indulge in the luxurious pampering that Hair Day has to offer.

OUR PROFESSIONALS

Hair Day is staffed with highly skilled professionals who excel in their respective roles, making them the perfect fit for the services we provide.

Pria, the proprietor of Hair Day, obtained her certification as a colorist and possesses expertise in innovative and personalized coloring methods. Alongside her is Garry, a skilled hairstylist, who co-founded the boutique salon with her.

Together, they bring a wealth of experience and talent to the establishment. Our team comprises seven dedicated employees who are committed to delivering exceptional service to our clients.

Our Staff include:

  • 2 Stylists
  • 3 Colorists
  • 1 Manicurist
  • 1 Make-up Artist

BOOKING APPOINTMENTS

Our cozy boutique hair salon has limited availability for coloring appointments each week.

Due to this constraint, our clients are encouraged to plan ahead and reserve their spot well in advance. This proactive approach helps us avoid overcrowding and ensures that each client receives personalized attention.

To secure their spot, clients can conveniently book appointments through our user-friendly website booking system.

Feel free to contact us any time incase of any enquiries about booking your appointment or otherwise and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
